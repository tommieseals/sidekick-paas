## 🎬 Demo Script (15-min call)

### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for taking the time. Before I show you anything, I'd love to understand — what's the most tedious, repetitive process your team deals with right now?"

### Discovery (3 min)
- How many hours/week does this take?
- How many people are involved?
- What happens when there's an error?
- Have you tried automating it before?

### Demo (7 min)
"Let me show you how [similar company] solved this exact problem..."
- Show workflow builder
- Show AI features
- Show integration with their existing tools

### Close (3 min)
"Based on what you told me, I estimate TaskBot could save your team [X] hours/week. Our Professional plan is $99/month. Want to start a free trial and test it on [their specific process]?"

## 📊 Pricing Objection Handlers

**"It's too expensive"**
→ "What's the hourly cost of your employees doing this manually? At $99/month, we pay for ourselves if we save just 3 hours."

**"We use Power Automate already"**
→ "How's that going? Most people tell us it's powerful but complex. TaskBot is built for business users, not developers."

**"We're not ready yet"**
→ "What would need to change for this to become a priority? Most of our customers say they wish they'd started 6 months earlier."

**"I need to talk to my team"**
→ "Totally understand. Would it help if I sent you a one-pager you could share? Or I'm happy to do a quick demo for the whole team."
