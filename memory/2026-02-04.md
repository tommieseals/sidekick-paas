### How It Works

**User Experience:**
1. Bot joins Teams meeting
2. Shows language selector: 🇩🇪 🇪🇸 🇺🇸 🇫🇷
3. Each person picks their language
4. Meeting starts
5. Klaus speaks German → Everyone else hears English/Spanish/etc.
6. Sarah speaks English → Everyone else hears German/Spanish/etc.
7. Real-time, continuous translation

**Technical:**
- Teams provides speaker identification (solves crosstalk!)
- Separate audio streams per speaker
- Translate to each listener's language
- Individual audio routing (UN-style)
- Visual captions as backup
